Why Sign Language Detection Matters

In India, over 63 million people with hearing impairments rely on sign language as their primary means of communication. Despite this, there remains a significant communication gap between the hearing and hearing-impaired communities.

Our Indian Sign Language Detection technology seeks to bridge this gap by using artificial intelligence and computer vision to interpret sign language in real-time, providing text and speech outputs that make communication more accessible.

By integrating this technology into everyday devices and public services, we can create more inclusive environments in schools, hospitals, government offices, and public spaces, ensuring that everyone has equal access to information and services.
